Quantitative Variable

The Quantitative Variables is First, “how much is the weekly allowance of Pisay Scholars? (not including their monthly stipend from school)”. The Pisay Upper class students's allowance is most likely Php 1,000 - Php 2,000. The Middle class is Php 500 - Php 1,000. Meanwhile, the Lower class most likely have a weekly allowance of less than Php 500. Second, The size of the individuals household (Family of 3 or less, Family of 4 to 6 and Family of 6 or more). In our data you can see that there are 3 individuals who answered the survey that belonged to a family of 2, 6 individuals belonged to a family of 3, 16 individuals belonged to a family of 4, 22 individuals belonged to a family of 5, 5 individuals belonged to a family of 6, Only 1 individual belonged to a family of 7, and 2 individuals belonged to a family of 8. Here we can see that the majority of the individuals belonged to a family of 5.

Qualitative Variable

The Qualitative Variables in our data is First, “What household type did you grow up with?” This is classified into 4 categories which are the Single-Parent Household, Single-Parent with Extended-Family Household, Two-Parent Household and an Extended-Family Household. There are 3 individuals who live in an Extended-Family household, 9 individuals who live in a Single-Parent household, 3 individuals who live in a Single-Parent with Extended-Family household and 39 individuals who live in a Two-Parent household. We can see that the majority of individuals who answered our survey belong to a Two-Parent household.
